Intro

I'm Alexander Harris, Technical Support Analyst and aspiring data scientist based in Miami, FL. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Florida International University and an Associate's degree in Ocean Engineering from Miami Dade College. With proficiency in Python, Tableau, SQL, and M365, I excel in process optimization, technology integration, and teamwork. My experience includes spearheading the deployment of an SSO application at ONE Sotheby’s International Realty and administering M365 licenses for a large team.

I'm also conversational in Spanish and Portuguese, which helps me connect with a broader range of clients and colleagues. In my previous role as a Sales Associate at Nespresso Lincoln Road, I designed a customer database that boosted our monthly sales by 10%. This experience honed my skills in CRM and data analysis. I'm passionate about leveraging technology to solve problems and enhance productivity.

Completed Projects

Exploring Airbnb Market Trends in Brisbane, QLD

This project involves a comprehensive data analysis of Airbnb listings in Australia, with a specific focus on Brisbane. The aim is to explore and visualize various aspects of Airbnb data to uncover trends, patterns, and insights that could be valuable for potential hosts and stakeholders.

Covid Data Exploration

This project uses an SQL script with advanced techniques to analyze COVID-19 data, revealing key insights on infection and death rates, vaccination trends, and regional impacts. This comprehensive approach helps in understanding and reporting on the pandemic's effects globally.

Covid Visualization Project

In this project, I built upon the insights from the SQL Data Exploration project to create a comprehensive Tableau dashboard. My primary goal for this initial Tableau visualization was to develop something clear and concise, focusing on effectively communicating key findings. This project also served as an introduction to the Tableau application, helping me to become familiar with its features and capabilities.

Whatsapp Data Scrapper

I completed a personal project with friends to log actions in a WhatsApp group chat. We tracked occurrences, calculated message percentages at various intervals, and identified instances of a member sending three consecutive messages. This project aimed to practice skills learned at FIU, explore Python's capabilities, and analyze its limitations. I found the process engaging and insightful.
